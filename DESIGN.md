# Biashara-Assistant Marketing Website Design

## Design Overview

A modern, mobile-first, one-page marketing website designed to help Kenyan small business owners quickly understand and adopt Biashara-Assistant. The design emphasizes trust, local relevance, and ease of use.

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Green**: `#25D366` (WhatsApp green) - CTAs, highlights, accents
- **Primary Green Dark**: `#1DA851` - Hover states, active elements
- **Primary Green Light**: `#4FE37A` - Subtle backgrounds, success states

### Neutral Colors
- **Background**: `#FFFFFF` (white) - Main background
- **Background Light**: `#F8F9FA` - Section backgrounds, card backgrounds
- **Background Subtle**: `#F0F2F5` - Alternating sections
- **Text Primary**: `#1F2937` - Headings, primary text
- **Text Secondary**: `#6B7280` - Body text, descriptions
- **Text Muted**: `#9CA3AF` - Helper text, labels
- **Border**: `#E5E7EB` - Dividers, card borders

### Accent Colors
- **Success**: `#10B981` - Positive indicators
- **Warning**: `#F59E0B` - Important notices
- **Info**: `#3B82F6` - Information highlights

## Typography

### Font Stack
- **Primary Font**: Inter (or system sans-serif: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, sans-serif)
- **Headings**: Bold, clear hierarchy
- **Body**: Regular weight, comfortable line height (1.6)

- **Hero Heading**: 32px (mobile) / 48px (desktop) - Bold, 1.2 line-height

- **Body Small**: 14px - Regular, 1.5 line-height
- **Button Text**: 16px - Semi-bold
- **Label**: 14px - Medium weight

## Mobile-First Considerations

### Breakpoints
- **Mobile**: < 768px (default, single column)
- **Tablet**: 768px - 1024px (2 columns where applicable)
- **Desktop**: > 1024px (3-4 columns, side-by-side layouts)

### Mobile Optimizations
- **Touch Targets**: Minimum 44px × 44px for buttons and links
- **Font Sizes**: Slightly larger on mobile for readability
- **Spacing**: Tighter on mobile, more generous on desktop
- **Images**: Optimized, lazy-loaded
- **Navigation**: Sticky header (optional) for easy scrolling
- **Forms**: Large inputs, easy-to-tap buttons
- **Cards**: Full-width on mobile, constrained on desktop

### Scroll Behavior
- **Smooth Scrolling**: Between sections
- **Sticky Elements**: Optional sticky CTA button on mobile
- **Progress Indicator**: Optional scroll progress bar at top

---

## Visual Hierarchy Principles

1. **Hero First**: Largest, most prominent section
2. **Clear Sections**: Each section has distinct background or spacing
3. **Progressive Disclosure**: Information builds from general to specific
4. **CTA Placement**: Primary CTAs in hero, pricing, form, and final CTA
5. **Visual Breathing Room**: Generous whitespace between sections
6. **Consistent Spacing**: Use spacing system consistently
7. **Color Accents**: Green used strategically for CTAs and highlights

## Accessibility Considerations

### Color Contrast
- **Text on White**: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio
- **Text on Green**: White text on green background
- **Interactive Elements**: Clear focus states

### Typography
- **Readable Font Sizes**: Minimum 16px for body text
- **Line Height**: Comfortable 1.5-1.6 for body text
- **Heading Hierarchy**: Clear H1, H2, H3 structure

### Interactive Elements
- **Focus States**: Visible green outline on focus
- **Button States**: Clear hover, active, disabled states
- **Form Labels**: Always visible, associated with inputs
- **Error Messages**: Clear, helpful error text

### Mobile Accessibility
- **Touch Targets**: Minimum 44px × 44px
- **Form Inputs**: Large, easy to tap
- **Scrollable Content**: Clear indication of scrollable areas

## Summary

This design creates a modern, mobile-first marketing website that:

1. **Immediately communicates value** in the hero section
2. **Builds trust** through clear benefits and local relevance
3. **Explains simply** how the product works
4. **Shows features** in an easy-to-scan format
5. **Relates to target audience** with business type examples
6. **Makes pricing clear** and accessible
7. **Removes friction** with a simple onboarding form
8. **Encourages action** with multiple strategic CTAs
9. **Maintains professionalism** while feeling friendly and local

The design uses WhatsApp green as the primary brand color, rounded cards for a modern feel, and generous whitespace for clarity. All copy is written in a friendly, trustworthy tone that speaks directly to Kenyan small business owners.
